About this course

Business Data Analytics is now a core competency for accounting and finance professionals. This KASNEB-accredited course provides the technical skills needed to analyze business data while maintaining the professional standards expected in accounting practice.

Designed specifically for CPA, CFA, and other KASNEB candidates, this course bridges the gap between traditional accounting practice and modern data analytics. You'll learn to apply analytical techniques to financial data, audit processes, and business decision-making scenarios.

The curriculum covers data collection from accounting systems, analysis using tools like Excel and Power BI, and interpretation of results for financial reporting and business strategy. Case studies focus on real-world accounting applications.

What you'll learn

  • Apply data analytics in auditing and financial reporting
  • Use Excel and Power BI for financial data analysis
  • Interpret analytical results for business decisions
  • Prepare data-driven reports for stakeholders
