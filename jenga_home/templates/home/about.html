About Thinking Post Training

Bridging accounting theory with real-world practice through tailored education.

Founded in 2025 by Evans Giture, Thinking Post Training was born from a passion to transform accounting education. With hands-on experience in audit, accounting, and business management, we deliver training that connects textbook concepts to practical applications.

Our Mission:

To empower professionals with skills that drive clarity and confidence in accounting and data analysis.

Our Edge:

Personalized attention, real-world case studies, and flexible learning paths.

Our Courses

We specialize in KASNEB-certified programs (like Business Data Analytics), ACCA training, and practical skill-building in Excel and data analysis. Each course is designed to:

  • Simplify complex accounting concepts
  • Integrate data analytics tools (Excel, Power BI)
  • Prepare students for real business challenges
